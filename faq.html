Q1)How do I enroll/register for SEEKHO online course?

Answer :-

step1- After opening the page click on login button.
step2- Enter the credentials required.
step3- After filling all the details click on register.
step4- Home page will be displayed then click on courses.
step5- select the course required to access it.

Q2)Is there a fee to join/register for an online course?

Answer :-

No. Joining/enrolling for an SEEKHO online course is free. Once enrolled, the videos can be accessed

Q3)What is the eligibility criterion for joining an online course?

Answer :-

There is no specific eligibility criterion for any of the SEEKHO online courses. The faculty of a particular course may recommend some basic knowledge of certain topics for a person to fully grasp the contents of a course.

Q4)Can I join for more than one course at a time?

Answer :-

Yes,you may join for any number of SEEKHO courses

Q5)What should I do after I sign up?

Answer :-

After sign-up, you have to watch the lecture videos
